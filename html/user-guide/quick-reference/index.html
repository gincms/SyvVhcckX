Glossary of InterTrader terms

  • Exchange: A Platform for trading currencies
  • Intertrade: Purchase a cryptocurrency on one exchange and sell it on another for profit
  • Coin pair, trading pair, crypocurrency pair, market: Combination of two cryptocurrencies that can be traded on an exchange
  • Ticker symbol: The alphanumeric code that is used to represent a cryptocurrency on exchanges
  • Coin-clash: Two or more cryptocurrencies that share the same ticker symbol
  • Buy/Bid price: The price traders are willing to pay for a cryptocurrency
  • Sell/Ask price: The price traders are willing to sell a cryptocurrency
  • Spread: The difference between the buy and sell price of a trading pair on an exchange
  • Buy spread/difference (%): The difference between the lowest and highest bid price across all exchanges
  • Sell spread/difference (%): The difference between the lowest and highest ask price across all exchanges
